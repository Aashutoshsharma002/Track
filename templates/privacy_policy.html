Privacy Policy

WorkFlowAI by AcadeWise is committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Data Collection

All data collected by WorkFlowAI remains exclusively on your device using browser localStorage:

  • Application usage data (which applications you use and for how long)
  • Window titles (to understand context of your activities)
  • System health metrics (CPU, memory, network usage, etc.)
  • Basic device information (device type, operating system)
  • User preferences and settings

2. No Server Storage

WorkFlowAI does not transmit or store any of your activity data on our servers. All processing, analysis, and storage happens directly in your browser.

3. Local Processing

Our machine learning models run entirely in your browser using TensorFlow.js. This means your data never leaves your device for processing or analysis.

4. User Control

You maintain complete control over your data:

  • You can delete all stored data at any time from the Settings page
  • You can selectively enable or disable specific types of tracking
  • You can export your data for your own records

5. Consent

WorkFlowAI will only track activities that you have explicitly consented to. You can modify your consent preferences at any time from the Settings page.

6. Security

Since your data remains on your device, the security of your information depends on your browser's security measures. We recommend keeping your browser updated and using secure passwords for your device.

7. Children's Privacy

WorkFlowAI is not intended for use by individuals under 16 years of age.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and, if significant, will be notified to you when you next use the application.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@acadewise.com
